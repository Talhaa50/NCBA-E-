    • Dear Prospective Students

    • For applying to the National College of Business Administration & Economics (NCBA&E),

    • Please note the following important information:

    • Online Registration: All candidates must complete the online registration process at https://ncbae.edu.pk/applyonline. After completing the registration, please attach a printout of the online registration form along with your admission form.

    • Documentation Required:

    Please prepare the following documents:

    • Four passport size photographs

    •  Copy of your National Identity Card (CNIC)

    • Matric/O Level marks sheet and certificate

    • Intermediate/A Level/F.A/F.Sc/ICS/I.Com marks sheet and certificate

    • Bachelor’s/B.A/BS(CS)/B.Com marks sheet and certificate

    •  Master’s/M.A/M.Sc/MCS/M.Com marks sheet and certificate

    •  If you are awaiting results, please attach your roll number slip

    •  For MBA-Executive candidates, a minimum of 3 years of job experience is required. Please provide a job experience letter.

    •  MBA-Executive candidates are required to clear an interview only; no test is required.

    Candidates who have appeared in the NTS exam do not need to take our entry test if they have achieved a minimum of 50 marks. Affidavit Requirement: Please submit an affidavit on a Rs. 50 stamp paper with the following undertaking:

    We look forward to receiving your application and assisting you throughout the admission process at NCBA&E.

    Best regards,

    Admissions National College of Business Administration & Economics

    Back in 1994 NCBA&E was founded as a private institute. Practicing excellence in teaching, learning and a close partnership with industry, its commitment and hard work earned a well-reputed place in the aspects of management studies.
