    The research program at NCBA&E is initiated with a vision to produce social scientists having an insight for conducting independent scholarly enquiry. The research program attempts to develop a new lifestyle in candidates that encourages them to continue lifelong learning even after completion of their Ph.D. degree.
    After acceptance for admission, the students have to collect their acceptance letters and deposit the registration fee (paid once, non-refundable). This initial registration is followed by the course registration for the imminent semester which is done by the guidance of the academic advisors. Few of the faculty members are appointed as Academic Advisors for undergraduate, graduate and post graduate students to guide them about courses in accordance with the semester and general academic supervision.

    A strong interdisciplinary research environment enables the student to establish links with their fellow researchers working on similar or related problems in other disciplines.

    NCBA&E is keen in developing links with industry, educational institutions and international bodies associated with pure and applied research, which is why NCBA&E has been organizing national and International conferences to provide a platform to its scholars for sharing research with fellow researchers around the world. We hope that students graduating from our institution will have a strong sense of commitment and a clear vision of the challenges faced by humanity in the 21st century. They would be working enthusiastically for the betterment of, not only the region, but the humanity at large.

    NCBA&E is the prominent institution in private sector of Pakistan that has a capacity to offer Ph.D. research opportunities in the following domains:

    ▪  Applied Statistics

    ▪  Applied Mathematics

    ▪  Business Administration

    ▪  Computer Science

    ▪  Economics

    ▪  Environmental Management
