    Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA Hons)

    INTRODUCTION

    If you aim to develop knowledge and skills in the field of business then study for Bachelors of Business Administration BBA (Hons). This degree not only broadens your horizon but also enables you to handle real-life business situations. It brings the newest practices so that you rise to the occasion.

    Courses you study in BBA are the core ones in the Business world such as Marketing, Accounting, Finance, HR, and Business communication. They not only help you develop fundamental Skills but also brush up your Soft skills so that you prosper.

    BBA (Hons) has 135 Credit Hours you need to cover over 4 years.

    Vision Statement

    NCBA&E aims to deliver the best-class knowledge to the students enrolled in the BBA Hons. program. Our business administration graduates possess an accurate understanding of diverse business management techniques. Learning under a highly efficient faculty, and by ample exposure to fruitful field experiences, our BBA graduates can compete with pride at national as well as global level. We believe in practical knowledge, and we are strongly envisioned to ensure a learning environment that speaks for the students’ satisfaction and capability.

    Objectives of the BBA (Hons) Program

    Our business graduates will possess and reflect an efficient understanding of all the processes involved in day-to-day business management. NCBA&E is strongly committed to ensuring the practical learning of the students. Our carefully designed roadmap enables the students to grasp theoretical as well as practical knowledge about the business terms including marketing, accounting finance, HR, and business communication. Many business graduates in the country struggle to make their way towards a promising career. What stands us apart is the clear understanding of how vitally important it is to empower on-field experiences for students while they complete their graduation.

    Admission Process

    For the ease of the candidates seeking admission in BBA (Hons.), here are all the important details regarding how to successfully apply for admission.

    Admissions are announced twice a year: Spring and Fall. To take admission in NCBA&E students have to get Admission Form from the Admission Office. Once filled, they have to attach all required documents necessary and submit it with the Admission Form to the Admission Office before the last date of admission, either by hand or by courier. Students seeking admission must also apply online link. Once both these procedures are done, students will be given a date for a short test of multiple-choice questions and a short essay (for Undergrad and MBA and MCS Programs only). Candidates with a minimum of 45% marks in their last certificates of exams are eligible to apply. Transfer Students from Government Chartered HEC approved institutes are welcome to transfer their credits to NCBA&E.

    Back in 1994 NCBA&E was founded as a private institute. Practicing excellence in teaching, learning and a close partnership with industry, its commitment and hard work earned a well-reputed place in the aspects of management studies.
