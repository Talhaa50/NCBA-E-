    HEALTH CARE FACILITY

    At our university, we are committed to ensuring the well-being and health security of our students through a comprehensive healthcare facility. Understanding the importance of accessible healthcare, the university provides health insurance cards that offer coverage up to 80,000 PKR per year. These insurance cards serve as a vital resource, empowering students with the assurance that they can access quality medical care whenever needed, without the burden of financial strain.

    Each insurance card is valid for a duration of one year, reflecting our dedication to maintaining consistent and reliable health coverage for our student body. This annual renewal process not only ensures continuous access to healthcare services but also allows us to update and improve the benefits offered, keeping pace with evolving medical needs and advancements in healthcare practices.

    The issuance of these insurance cards underscores our university’s commitment to fostering a supportive and holistic educational environment. By prioritizing the health and well-being of our students, we aim to empower them to focus on their academic pursuits with confidence, knowing that their healthcare needs are comprehensively covered.

    Moreover, our healthcare facility extends beyond insurance coverage. We provide a range of medical services and resources on campus, including regular health check-ups, consultations with qualified healthcare professionals, and access to specialized treatments when required. This integrated approach to healthcare not only promotes physical well-being but also supports the overall mental and emotional health of our students.

    In essence, our university’s healthcare facility and insurance program are designed to be proactive, responsive, and student-centric. We strive to create an environment where every student feels supported in their health journey, enabling them to thrive academically and personally during their time with us.
