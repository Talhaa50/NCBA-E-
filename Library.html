    Discover Our Latest Library Activities

    Listen at NCBAE

    We at NCBAE Library are excited to offer a wide selection of audiobooks for our patrons. Our audiobooks provide a convenient and engaging way to enjoy your favorite books. You can borrow them to listen at home, during your commute, or while exercising. Additionally, we have set up comfortable listening stations within the library, allowing you to enjoy audiobooks on-site. This initiative is part of our commitment to making literature accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Explore our collection today and find your next great listen!

    Collaborative Areas

    At NCBAE Library, we provide dedicated spaces for group discussions and research. Whether you're working on a collaborative project or conducting research with peers, our library offers comfortable and well-equipped areas designed to support effective teamwork. You and your crew can reserve private study rooms or use open areas for brainstorming and group study. In addition, our library provides access to various research tools and resources to help you and your team succeed. Join us to make the most of these collaborative opportunities and enhance your academic experience together.

    Career Guidance

    At NCBA Library, we offer a weekly Career Development class designed to support your professional growth. Each session is led by a knowledgeable professor who provides valuable guidance on career advancement, including resume building, job search strategies, and interview skills. These classes are a great opportunity to receive expert advice and practical tips to enhance your career prospects. Join us every weekend to gain insights and skills that can help you achieve your professional goals.
