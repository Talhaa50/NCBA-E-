    No BackPack Day

    We organized a ‘No Backpack Day’ where many students in the department participated cheerfully and depicted numerous alternatives to the traditional school bag. The substitutes were not limited to bindles and hangars, but included cauldrons and weightlifting belts. The day was very much adored and relished as students were eager to show the ideas they had come up with.

    Orientation Week

    Orientation week, often regarded as the gateway to university life, is an exhilarating period filled with excitement and anticipation. It serves as a pivotal moment for new students, offering a comprehensive introduction to campus resources, academic expectations, and the vibrant community they are about to join. During orientation week, students embark on a journey of exploration, where they familiarize themselves with campus facilities, participate in informative sessions on university policies and services, and engage in social activities designed to foster connections with peers and faculty members alike.

    Art Exibition

    The university art exhibition is a culmination of creative expression and scholarly inquiry, where the campus transforms into a vibrant showcase of artistic talent and intellectual exploration. Organized by students, faculty, and staff, this event not only highlights the diverse artistic endeavors within the university community but also bridges disciplines, cultures, and perspectives.

    Northern Tours

    The Northern tour organized by NCBA offers students a transformative journey through some of the country's most breathtaking landscapes and culturally rich regions. This expedition not only serves as a recreational adventure but also as an educational experience, providing participants with a deeper understanding of Pakistan's diverse geography, history, and cultural heritage.
